What is myCASA?

myCASA is an online portal where you can manage your details, information and interactions with CASA.

Currently you can access the following services in myCASA:

  • Apply for an Aviation Reference Number (ARN) online
  • Register your drones/remotely piloted aircraft
  • Gain accrediation for operationg of drones/remotely piloted aircraft
  • Update your contact and personal information

Other new services are also currently being developed.
